\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Holds essential information about a trade, including order ID, price, and quantity. 

\doxylink{struct_trade_info}{Trade\+Info} encapsulates the core details of a trade and is typically used within a \doxylink{class_trade}{Trade} object to represent either the bid or ask side of a transaction.
